## What I'm Building

### [SovEcom](https://github.com/asifwanders/SovEcom) — EU-first headless ecommerce
The open-source alternative to Shopify, built for European developers and agencies from day one. AGPL-3.0. Full EU VAT compliance, GDPR tooling, Stripe payments, a sandboxed module system, and a Next.js storefront — all in a single `docker compose up`. Currently at v1.0.2 and actively shipping.

`TypeScript` · `NestJS` · `PostgreSQL` · `Next.js 15` · `Docker` · `AGPL-3.0`

### [SovLens](https://github.com/asifwanders/SovLens) — Local AI for your photo library
Drop a folder. Ask questions. Get answers. SovLens runs entirely on your machine — no cloud, no accounts, no data leaving your device. It understands your photos and videos using local AI models.

`Python` · `Local AI` · `GPL-3.0`
